We had two papers accepted and presented at the Artificial Life conference on topics in co-design of robots! 
One paper on ["Understanding the Navigation of Robot Morphology Spaces with Local Optima Network Analysis"](https://direct.mit.edu/isal/proceedings-pdf/isal2025/37/42/2567101/isal.a.870.pdf) with the awesome Sarah Thompson on new approaches and analysis of the design space of robot morphologies. 
The second paper with my collaborateur [Kishan Nagiredla](https://experts.deakin.edu.au/57459-kishan-nagiredla) on ["COGENT: Co-design of Robots with Generative Flow Networks"](https://direct.mit.edu/isal/proceedings-pdf/isal2025/37/77/2567081/isal.a.905.pdf) using Generative Flow networks to co-optimize the embodiment and behaviour of robotic agents.
